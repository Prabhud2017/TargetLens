Privacy Policy for Target Lens AI

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

1. Information Collection

Target Lens AI is a developer tool designed to debug Adobe Target implementations. The extension processes network request headers, cookies (such as mbox and Adobe Experience Cloud IDs), and XDM data payloads.

2. Data Usage

All data processing occurs locally on your machine. The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or browsing data to external servers or the developer. The data is used solely to display real-time debugging information within the extension's interface.

3. Permissions Justification

  • webRequest / declarativeNetRequest: Used to intercept and display Adobe Target delivery and interact calls.
  • storage: Used to save your local extension settings and preferences.
  • cookies: Used to display active mbox and visitor IDs for debugging purposes.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We do not use any third-party tracking or analytics within the extension itself.

5. Contact

If you have questions, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
