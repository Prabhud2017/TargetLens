TARGET LENS AI

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Target Lens AI is a specialized developer tool built to assist in debugging Adobe Target implementations. We value your privacy and have designed this extension to be as transparent and secure as possible.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Target Lens AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information intercepted (such as mbox calls, network request headers, and XDM payloads) is processed locally and in real-time on your device for the sole purpose of displaying debug information.

3. Permissions Usage
  • Web Request: To identify and parse Adobe Target network calls.
  • Storage: To remember your preferred extension settings (e.g., UI theme or filters).
  • Cookies: To display active visitor IDs and mbox cookies essential for troubleshooting activity delivery.
4. Security

Because no data leaves your local environment, your Adobe Experience Cloud credentials and visitor data remain private within your browser session.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts.
